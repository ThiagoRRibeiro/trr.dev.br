Squad Hub

The all-in-one mobile platform designed to help recreational and amateur sports teams manage their squads, organize matches, and track performance.

Get it on Google Play Download on App Store

APP FEATURES

Squad & Player Management

Centralized directory of the team, making it simple to keep track of who is available to play, replacing scattered contacts.

Match Organization & Lineup Editor

Schedule matches and visually drag and drop players into opposing teams with an intuitive interface.

AI Auto Balance Lineups

Automatically divide players into highly balanced, competitive teams with a single tap using a smart algorithm.

Player Statistics & Season Tracking

Log goals, assists, and wins to create professional-level leaderboards and track performance across seasons.

Payment Tracking

Easily track the financial status of every match, ensuring no personal financial shortfalls when renting fields.

Quick Match Generator

Rapidly create temporary matches and generate fair lineups for spontaneous pick-up games.
